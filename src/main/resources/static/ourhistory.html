Our History

MTI was a notebook specialist since 1990 and expanded its service into digital marketing field at 1998. In the fast-paced, ever-demanding business world, the ability to access information anywhere, anytime is crucial. MTI specializes in developing mobile solution that provided global reach and universal access to information and resources anytime, anyplace. At that time,MTI’s philosophy was that reliability, quality and service take precedence when providing notebook computer solutions. MTI also realized the world demand of CD-R and DVD+R are currently experiencing an explosive growth, fueled by the falling in media and recorder prices in 1997. The worldwide consumption of CD-R media was 200 million in 1997, which increased to 650 million in 1998, and is forecasted to more than 3 billion in 2003. MTI engaged in the business of import, sales and distribution of Hewlett Packard (HP) CD-R, CD-RW, DVD+R and DVD+RW media products and other related activities throughout the United States, Canada and South America. MTI started from 2003, in the North America, to promote HP CD-R and DVD+R media in the professional duplicator market. Due to high quality from the HP media and professional service provided by MTI, MTI had established a strong distribution in N. America. 
Since 2006, MTI began to establish a strong relationship with a number of major Canadian retailers by providing OEM products, services and solutions. Having a good relationship with overseas manufactures, MTI was successfully introducing competitive products to the Canadian Market via mass-market channels with private label products. MTI had provided private labeled computer accessories products and consumer electronic products to major retailers and distribute across Canada.
Since 2012, MTI had become a western logistic office & service center for an international well-known Japanese Household appliances manufacturer – Tiger products. MTI was responsible to provide sales &services of rice cookers, water dispensers and thermo mugs for the major supermarkets e.g. Costco, Canadian Tires, TNT, H-Mart and Overwaitea Foods and as well independent resellers.
Building upon on its established distribution channel, MTI formed an Import Food Division in 2013. In addition to general food items, Mti has also obtained Produce & Seafood import licenses. Currently, we have imported directly from some well-known overseas manufacturers. Among from Asian countries, we have imported over 500 items and continued to expand our product ranges.
In 2016, we have signed an agreement with UK Tesco and Mti has become the sole distributor of UK Tesco products in Canada. Currently we have distributed 105 Tesco items over 120 stores and continued to expand on both products and store numbers.
