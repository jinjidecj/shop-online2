Our History

    MTI was a notebook specialist since 1990 and expanded its service into digital marketing field at 1998.
    From 2003, MTI started to promote HP CD-R and DVD+R media in the professional duplicator market in the North America and established a strong distribution in N. America.
    Since 2006, MTI began to establish a strong relationship with a number of major Canadian retailers by providing OEM products and services, then successfully introducing private label products to the Canadian Market via mass-market channels.
    Since 2012, MTI had become a western logistic office & service center for a well-known Japanese Household appliances manufacturer – Tiger products.
    In 2013, MTI formed an Import Food Division. In addition to general food items, MTI has also obtained Produce & Seafood import licenses.
    In 2016, we have signed an agreement with UK Tesco and MTI has become the sole distributor of UK Tesco products in Canada.
    Currently, among from Asian countries, we have imported over 500 items and continued to expand our product ranges.
