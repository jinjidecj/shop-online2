ONLY ONE BRAND

Hetbahn was launched in December 1996, creating the market for cooked rice.
This brand represents CJ’s spirit of ONLY ONE.
Hetbahn means freshly harvested and cooked rice.
The rice is polished through strict management of material at CJ’s mill and cooked within a day. It is packaged in a sterile environment to keep the taste of freshly cooked.
Hetbahn tries to move forward as a representative food brand by creating customer satisfaction and new products with the best quality and advanced technology.
